THE TRUTH HIDDEN IN PLAIN SIGHT

In January 2024, the court unsealed over 900 pages of documents from the lawsuit Virginia Giuffre v. Ghislaine Maxwell. The release exposed the intricate web of power, money, and influence that protected one of the most prolific sex offenders in history.

THE TIMELINE

2005

The First Investigation

Palm Beach police begin investigating Epstein after reports of abuse.

2008

The Non-Prosecution Agreement

Epstein pleads guilty to lesser state charges in a controversial deal involving Alex Acosta.

2015

Giuffre v. Maxwell

Virginia Giuffre sues Ghislaine Maxwell for defamation. This lawsuit generates the documents released in 2024.

2019

Arrest & Death

Epstein is arrested on federal sex trafficking charges. He dies in jail a month later.

2024

The Unsealing

Judge Loretta Preska orders the release of sealed documents, revealing names and depositions previously hidden.
